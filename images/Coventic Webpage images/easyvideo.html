Easy Video ‑ Product Videos

Would like to increase your conversion rate with fancy videos illustrating your products' features and benefits? But your Theme does not support videos for the products? No problem, just install our app and you'll be able to assign YouTube or Vimeo video to any Product Picture!

Enable YouTube videos for your theme

YouTube videos added to the product gallery does not appear on your Store front? Theme needs to be updated, but you can't loose customizations or 3rd party apps settings? No problem, just install EasyVideo and your theme is supporting YouTube vides now!

How it works

After installing our app you'll be able to add YouTube video to your product gallery as usual, or just add a link to YouTube or Vimeo video in your Product Image ALT, and the app will show this video instead of an enlarged image (Product Image itself will be still used for the thumbnail), or in a fancy popup window - depending on your settings.

You even don't need to grab the embed code for the video, just copy/paste a link to the video from your browser, and Easy Video will do the rest for you!

Is my theme supported?

We did our best to make Easy Video work with the most themes out of the box. Nevertheless, some themes will require additional configuration via app settings. If the app will not display videos immediately after installation, you may configure it manually in app settings, or ask our support to do that for you.

Installation

Due to the nature of Easy Video app, some themes may require additional app configuration. That's why we added 7 days free trial, so you may feel free to test app with your theme. If you think the app is not working properly, please contact our staff and we'll do our best to make our video app work on your Store.
